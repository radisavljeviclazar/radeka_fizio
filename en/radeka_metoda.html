My story

If every massage tells a story i hold an entire library

With over 30.000 successfully conducted treatments, every page of my story reveals unique experience and dedication, I began with the Partizan cycling club and the Serbian cycling federation, where i learned how to care for athletes under the pressure of competition. Five years in a psychiatric clinic deepened my understanding of the body and mind, while working with football clubs abroad prepared me for the dynamics of sport injuries and recovery. As a member of the Hilandar Medical Society, i connected tradition with moder therapeutic techniques. My work with monks in the holy Monastery of Hilandar led to deep insights into the unbreakable connection between body, soul, and spirit. Practical experience at the military medical academy provided me with a strong medical foundation, validated the effectiveness of my approach, and enabled the application of techniques that are proven in practice and recognized by experts. The foundations stands as a guarantee that my method is aligned with the highest professional and medical standards. Private trainings and working directly with athletes in the field further shaped me, allowing me to develop my own therapeutic program - Fr Physio Radeka. Over the past 15 years, this method has helped countless clients restore balance and wellbeing. Every massage i give is not just a tretment, but a part of a unique journey that i continue to write with every new challenge.
